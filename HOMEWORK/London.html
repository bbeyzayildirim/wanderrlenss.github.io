London: Royal City

London is one of the most important metropolises in the world, combining history, culture and modernity. Located on the banks of the River Thames, this charming city is known for its landmarks such as Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum.

Big Ben: Icon of London

Big Ben is one of the symbolic buildings of London and is located as part of the Palace of Westminster. With its bell ringing every hour and its magnificent architecture, Big Ben is one of the most well-known clock towers in the world.
