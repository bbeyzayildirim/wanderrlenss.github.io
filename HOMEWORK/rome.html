Rome: Literary City

Rome is one of the most impressive cities in the world with its historical and cultural richness. With its ancient ruins, magnificent architecture and breathtaking works of art, visiting Rome offers an unforgettable experience.

Colosseum: Ancient Arena

The Colosseum is the symbol of Rome and the largest amphitheater of antiquity. This enormous structure, used for gladiator fights and other events, reflects the power and magnificence of the Roman Empire.
